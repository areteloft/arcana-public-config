About the Project

A small indie tarot project.

I have my own style of tarot reading and interpretation, and this project started from a simple question: how much of that can be translated into a digital format without losing the feeling of it?

What I wanted was not just a tool that gives instant answers, but something that keeps the act of drawing cards, the atmosphere around the reading, and a quiet sense of immersion.

Part of the project has been thinking about how a personal reading style can be expressed digitally, while still feeling intentional rather than mechanical.

What I’m trying to make

The focus is on three things: a UI where you directly draw the cards yourself, a distinct world and mood around the reading, and interpretations that feel reflective rather than flat.
